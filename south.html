Meenakshi Temple, Madurai

The Meenakshi Temple complex is literally a city - one of the largest of its kind in India and undoubtedly one of the oldest too.
The temple grew with the contribution of each dynasty and victorious monarchs, into an enormous complex extending over an area of 65000 Sq m.
